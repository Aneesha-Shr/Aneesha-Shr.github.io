Budget Bridge

Need to manage a large grant for research? Want to prevent accidentally going over budget, or illegal purchases? Look no further than Budget Bridge, a preliminary software designed to help you with your budgeting needs. This full stack software uses the power of SQL, Python, and React.js to create a web-based interface where users can create categories and subcategories of purchase, rules for spending requests, lists of approved vendors, etc. It's the ideal tool for a PI or co-PIs who need help managing larger projects! Interested in purchasing or trying a demo? Email me.

Pegasus Capital Management

Using market indicators and the power of python, the Pegasus Capital Management team has created a stock trading algorithm which automatically buys, sells, and trades on the market, with a current annual return of 30%. We utilize volatility indicators and our special risk management formulas in order to dynamically allocate percentages of our capital towards buys, and a stop-loss system which minimizes loss and ensure disciplined exits. Our design has placed us as a top 5 finalist in the national Southeastern Hedge Fund Competition.

Gender Bias Indicator

Interested in the gender bias in the news sources your consuming? I've created a web scraping program using R, where you can input the URL of any news article, and recieve a pie or bar chart that shows the gender bias, calculated using a list of gendered words compiled by a team of graduate researchers at MIT. Use this product to find trends in gender bias among websites, or in your own writing!
