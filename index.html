Internships and Opportunities

Idaho Human Rights Commissions

The Idaho Human Rights Commission is a branch of the Idaho state government and the federal EEOC. They work tirelessly to investigate any complaints of employment discrimination on the basis of race, sex, age, disability, color and national origin, religion, and retaliation. As an intern I worked as an investigator, compiling evidence and making preliminary decisions on cases. Through this work, I enhanced my ability to write complex legal arguments in an easy-to-read manner, and using simple language to make a strong case.

Undergraduate Research Assistant

Wind turbines used to be the face of clean energy, and were hailed the power source of the future. However, each turbine is extremely expensive, and has a short lifespan due to unknown reasons (they tend to catch on fire). Research in this area to improve turbines is extremely limited, as wind turbines are so large they don't fit into laboratory environments, and so costly using them for research is a poor investment. I worked with Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories and the University of Idaho Center for Secure and Dependable Systems to create a miniature model of a wind turbine, which is cost effective, easily tested in a laboratory, and has special software which outputs data to researchers as the turbine runs test scenarios.

During the fall semester of 2023, I had the honor and privilege of serving as a Senator for the Associated Students of the University of Idaho. UMass Amherst Fine Arts Center - Stagehand

One of the most fun jobs I've had the pleasure of working is as a stagehand at the Fine Arts Center on the University of Massachusetts - Amherst campus. Through this job I learned how to set up and hang stage lights, fun stage terminology, how to climb up and down the sides of a stage to manipulate the curtain, and basic stage audio. I also learned many translatable skills, like how to work quietly and efficiently with a team during a performance, and staying on my feet for 6-8 hour shifts.
